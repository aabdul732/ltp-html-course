HTML5 Specialist Program

The HTML5 Specialist Program is designed to provide developers with proof their coding skills. The program includes a course and exam. Once you complete both course an the exam, you will receive the HTML5 Specialist Designation.

Upon earning the HTML5 Specialist Designation, you can display the HTML5 Specialist badge on your resume, portfolio and on your linkedin profile. You will also recive a digital certificate that you can print and frame.
